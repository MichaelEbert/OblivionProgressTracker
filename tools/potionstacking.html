Potion Stacking Calculator

Select a single positive spell effect, and get the three best potions for stacking that effect as much as possible, along with the sequence to consume the potions in. Optionally filter out certain types of ingredients.

This tool is designed for use after reaching level 100 Alchemy and acquiring a full Master-tier set of Alchemy equipment. Your results may vary if you do not meet these requirements.
