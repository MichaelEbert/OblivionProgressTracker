Oblivion 100% Speedrun Route (V3)

Made by Ben Songster (MeemawHustlin) with help from the PRCLive community.

Pregame
  1. Enter the console commands “sdt 12” and “tdt” before the run starts, and make sure your wait timer is set to 1 hour. If the game crashes at any point, enter the “sdt 12” and “tdt” console commands again.
  2. Move the difficulty slider to the easiest setting (all the way left)
  3. Make your character a Male Redguard.
  4. Time starts as soon as you click "Continue" on the first tutorial box that appears. You are not allowed to move before that happens.
Tutorial
  1. Click continue on the quest pop-up to start the run. Press caps lock to always be sprinting, and press F3 to bring up the spell menu.
  2. Hotkey Flare and equip Adrenaline Rush.
  3. Cast Adrenaline Rush and Save Clip through the right front edge of the NE archway.
  4. When you clip through the wall, run to the east until you spawn back in bounds. This is called Void Warping. If the clip doesn’t work, try setting the quicksave up again and loading again.
  5. Jump up into the east archway and Save Clip through the seam between the two walls. Continue to run east after you clip until the black box is off the top of the screen, then wait until you Void Warp.
  6. Take the Chameleon and Flash Bolt scrolls, as well as the lockpicks and Iron Key off the Goblin corpse. Make a save. Make sure to write down the number of the save. It will be referred to as PermaKey_Save later.
  7. Open the door so that the popup shows up about using the Iron Key, and press escape to load the PermaKey_Save you just made. This glitches the game into allowing you to open most doors without needing to pick the lock.
  8. Continue linearly until you get past the square room with eight pillars. Pick up the Tomato, Cheese, and Rusty Iron Shield in the center of these pillars. Then hug the right wall to find a chest just past the next area with the light shafts. Take the Potions of Healing and Weak Potion of Sorcery from it.
  9. Continue linearly to the next area with a goblin. Take the Novice Mortar and Pestle from on top of the broken crate.
  10. Continue to the main large area with several goblins. Take the Repair Hammer and 18 gold from the locked chest by the Goblin Shaman.
  11. Continue until you get to the Imperial Subterrane. Fall down and use Flare to kill the Mythic Dawn Agent up on the SW ledge.
  12. Head SE and punch the Emperor six times to make them chase you. Run back to the door in the main area and yield when Glenroy is close enough to you.
  13. Kill the Mythic Dawn Agent when the door opens, then wait for the next door to open and kill the other Mythic Dawn Agents that appear.
  14. Run up to the Emperor and wait for him to talk to you.
    a. Advance predetermined dialogue. b. Select The Steed as your birthsign. c. Advance all predetermined dialogue.
  15. Talk to Baurus, who will give you a torch.
  16. Use your hotkey to equip Flare again. Hit the Emperor once to get them to chase you, then run ahead and kill any Mythic Dawn Agents on your way. Yield to the Emperor when you are by the door to the next area.
  17. Make sure Baurus spawns in the next area when you go through the door. If he doesn’t you may need to go back through the door and enter the zone again.
  18. In the next area, hug the right wall and jump to the ledge across the gap. Save Clip facing west through the door. As soon as you clip through, curve around to the left and run SE until you Void Warp.
  19. Head SE down the hall and Flare the Mythic Dawn Agent.
  20. Head back and go through the Iron Gate and talk to Baurus.
    a. Advance predetermined dialogue. b. Make a custom class with the following: i. Specialization: Magic ii. Favored Attributes: Strength and Speed iii. Major skills: Acrobatics, Alteration, Conjuration, Destruction, Illusion, Mysticism, and Restoration c. Advance predetermined dialogue.
  21. Continue linearly through the rest of the tutorial, going back through the Iron Gate you opened.
  22. When you exit the sewer, change your race to a Female High Elf.
  23. tutorial is now complete.
Imperial City Market District
  1. Fast travel to the Imperial City Market District and head WSW into the first archway, then hug the right wall until you find the Mystic Emporium.
  2. Wait until 8am, then talk to Calindil.
  3. Haggle to 50% sell value, then sell the Chameleon and Flash Bolt scrolls. Buy 2 of any Absorb scroll that costs 14 or less, and 1 of another Absorb scroll of the same or less cost. Dupe the 1 stack using the 2 stack, pick one up, then repeat and pick up all scrolls so that you have 3 of one scroll and 2 of another. Now, dupe the stacks back and forth until you have 512 of one and 768 of another.
  4. From the 768 stack, sell 200, 200, then 68 scrolls back to Calindil. From the 512 stack, sell 200, 200, then 62 scrolls. You should have 300 of one type, and 50 of another, as well as over 4,300 gold at the end.
  5. Read The Black Arts on Trial [Mysticism] skill book on the counter.
  6. Head SE to Red Diamond Jewelry. Talk to Hamlof and buy the Brass Ring. Dupe about 300 lockpicks.
Mages Guild Pt. 1
Bravil Recommendation
  1. Fast travel to Bravil.
  2. Wait until 1am and head between the first two buildings on the left to buy 1 Skooma from Nordinor.
  3. Drop the Skooma and pick it back up. Dupe the Skooma with the 50 scroll stack, and then the 300 scroll stack. Hotkey Skooma.
    Use the following method to increase your movement speed everywhere you go:
    How to Drink Extra Skooma:
    1. Drink 4 Skooma using the hotkey.
    2. Go into the inventory and drink 4 more Skooma.
    Be careful not to lose much of your fatigue bar when farming Acrobatics. If you pass out, wait an hour.

  4. Head SW towards the Mages Guild. Stop at the Chapel on your way.
  5. In the Chapel Hall downstairs, read 2920, Rain’s Hand (v4) [Restoration] on the left shelf, and take the Nirnroot from the south room.
  6. Go to the Mages Guild, wait until 9am, and talk to Kud-Ei.
    1. Dialogue: Join the Mages Guild.
    2. Dialogue: Yes, I want to join the guild. (This starts Join the Mages Guild)
    3. Dialogue: Recommendation.
    4. Dialogue: I’m ready.
    5. Dialogue: Varon Vamori.
    6. Dialogue: Mage’s Staff.
  7. Activate this quest in your journal.
  8. Follow Quest Marker to talk to Varon Vamori.
    1. Bribe to 65+ disposition.
    2. Dialogue: Ardaline.
    3. Dialogue: Mage’s Staff.
  9. Buy Spark from Delphine Jend.
  10. Take at least 4 Cheap Wine near the east corner of the ground floor.
  11. Take the Lesser Soul Gem from the SW bookshelf behind the back counter.
  12. Fast travel to Imperial City Talos Plaza District.
  13. Head NE, second door on the right to get to Soris Arenim’s House.
  14. Talk to Soris Arenim.
    1. Bribe to 70+ disposition.
    2. Dialogue: Mage’s Staff.
    3. Dialogue: It’s a deal.
  15. Fast travel to Quest Marker to talk to Kud-Ei.
    1. Dialogue: Mage’s Staff.
  16. [quest] Bravil Mages Guild is now complete.
Anvil Recommendation
  1. Fast travel to Anvil Main Gate.
  2. Head south to the Mages Guild and talk to Carahil.
    1. Dialogue: Recommendation.
    2. Dialogue: Yes, I’m ready.
    3. Dialogue: Go on.
  3. Fast travel to Horse Whisperer Stables and make your way to the Quest Marker to talk to Arielle Jurard.
  4. Follow Quest Marker to talk to Christophe Marane.
    1. Dialogue: Bed.
    2. Dialogue: I’m a merchant.
    3. Dialogue: I’ll take it.
  5. You will automatically be approached by Caminalda.
    1. Dialogue: Yes, that’s right.
  6. Go upstairs to your bed and wait 1 hour. Arielle Jurard will appear. Talk to her.
  7. Sleep in the bed for 1 hour.
  8. Heads towards the Quest Marker until Caminalda approaches you.
  9. Kill Caminalda and fast travel to Quest Marker to talk to Carahil.
    1. Dialogue: Rogue Mage.
  10. [quest] Anvil Mages Guild is now complete.
  11. Retrieve the Dragon’s Tongue and Nightshade in the upstairs library. The ingredients are on a table through the door in the NW corner of the library.
Cheydinhal Recommendation
  1. Fast travel to Cheydinhal West Gate.
  2. Head SE around the back of the first building to enter the well.
    1. If this door does not open, load the PermaKey_Save and do the Perma Key glitch.
  3. Get the Nirnroot at the south end of the well.
  4. On the west end of the well, take the Ring of Burden off of Vidkun’s corpse.
  5. Exit the well and go around to the front of the south building to enter the Mages Guild.
  6. Head east down into the basement.
  7. Read The Firsthold Revolt [Mysticism] on the bookshelf next to the crystal ball at the bottom of the stairs.
  8. Head west and open the locked door, then open the locked drawers and take the Black Soul Gems.
    1. If this door does not open, load the PermaKey_Save and do the Perma Key glitch.
  9. Talk to Falcar. He may be anywhere in the building and is wearing black.
    1. Dialogue: Recommendation.
    2. Dialogue: Yes, I’m ready.
  10. Wait for the quest to update and find and talk to Deetsan. She is Argonian.
    1. Dialogue: Recommendation.
  11. Wait for the quest to update and talk to Deetsan again. Drop the Ring of Burden.
  12. [quest] Cheydinhal Mages Guild is now complete.
  13. After finishing the quest, follow the map to the right to get to the Mage Stone between 6pm-6am. Start from the stable outside of Cheydinhal, and make sure to discover Vahtacen, the cave SE past Harlun’s Watch on your way there.
